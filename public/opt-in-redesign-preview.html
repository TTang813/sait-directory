01 Purpose of the Directory

The SAIT Practitioner Directory is a voluntary, opt-in service for SAIT members in good standing to display their professional details and specialisations to the public.

02 Eligibility & Membership

Only current SAIT members in good standing are eligible. Listings may be removed if membership lapses.

03 What We Display

Always shown: full name, PR number, region, specialisation(s).

  • Your company name — only if you choose to include it
  • Your personal contact details — never displayed
08 Data Protection & POPIA
Legal Basis — POPIA

By opting in, you consent to SAIT processing and publicly displaying your name, PR number, region, and specialisation(s) as described above, under POPIA.
